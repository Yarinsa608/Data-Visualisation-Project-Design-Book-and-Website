Insights from the Data

Camera Enforcement Dominates

Our analysis reveals that fixed and mobile cameras account for 72% of all fines issued, highlighting the growing role of automated enforcement in road safety.

Prime Offending Age

Adults aged 26-39 received 40.8% of all fines, suggesting this demographic may need targeted education about mobile phone use while driving.

Seasonal Patterns

Fines peak during March and November, possibly correlating with increased enforcement campaigns during holiday periods.

Key Takeaway

While automated enforcement is effective at detecting mobile phone offences, the relatively low arrest count (only 5) suggests most offenders are receiving fines rather than facing more serious consequences.
